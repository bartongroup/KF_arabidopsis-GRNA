    to it (in our case it was /homes/kfroussios/Projects/AtWTdist/). All commands are executed from here afterwards. Absolute paths required in some

    match your system.


[1] You may need to configure the PYTHONPATH and PERL5LIB environment variables
    to include the rnascripts/, grnascripts/ and grnascripts/Modules/
    directories. Several scripts also provide parameters with
    which to tell them where certain dependencies and executables are.
    Also the PLPLOT_LIB and DRMAA_LIBRARY_PATH environment
    variables may need to be set to where these external modules are in your system. You may need to obtain third-party packages for your
    system/R/Perl/Python/GridEngine installations, such as drmaa (python,perl),
    the various DGE tools (R), PDL and PLplot (perl), numpy/scipy (python) etc.
    Error messages will usually inform you of missing packages.
